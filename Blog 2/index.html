Kaas Plateau Ban 2020 Covid19

Every year around September, thousands of tourists visit the Kaas plateau to witness the flower festival. But this year, tourism is banned everywhere due to the covid19. Forest officials have appealed that no one should come to the Plateau this year. Information boards have been put up on the Satara to Kas Pathar route. The local authority has issued a notice. If any person from outside Satara district comes to Kaas plateau in violation of this Covid19 ban, action has also been ordered against him. The flowering season has begun on the Kaas Plateau. Kindly cooperate with the forest department, Due to this year's Covid19 Kaas Pathar ban, no one should come to the Kaas Plateau 2020.

Kas Pathar 2020 Banned by the Satara Forest Department has appealed to the people not to visit the Kaas Plateau this year as it is forbidden to visit tourist places due to Coronavirus. The Forest department has initiated action against some tourists for violating the ban as the flower festival started on the Kaas Plateau with the rainy season almost coming to an end.
